**Atlas Adventures - Travel Agency Website**
A modern, responsive travel agency website built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Atlas Adventures helps travelers discover breathtaking destinations and create unforgettable memories around the world.
**Features**

Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
Interactive Navigation: Smooth scrolling navigation with mobile menu
Tour Categories: Forest Adventures, Mountain Climbing, Beach Vacations, and City Tours
Destination Showcase: Featured destinations with pricing and duration
Customer Reviews: Slider showcasing client testimonials
Blog Section: Latest travel news and tips
Contact Form: Easy trip planning with location and date selection
